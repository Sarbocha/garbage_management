Our Services

We have different plans and options according to your need.

Regular Pickup

In this plan we pickup garbage from your house on a regular basis. This is suitable for a single household with garbage less than 50kg.

$9.99

Montly Pickup

In this package we will provide you a huge dust collector and collect your garbage once a month.

$13.05

Community Pickup

Instead for a single house you can get the service for a lot cheaper. You will be provided with good discout and offers.
